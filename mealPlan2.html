fall 2022 meal plans

Our Meal Plans are perfect for every Panther's lifestyle, find a meal plan that best fits your eating lifestyle while at FIU.  There are plans that offer unlimited access to 8th Street Campus Kitchen (an all-you-care-to-eat restaurant in the Graham Center), a Weekly allotment of meal swipes, or a Block of meals per semester. In addition to an allotment of dining dollars, PLUS a discount on regular price at all Panther Dining locations.We know our guests have different food preferences, and our team of culinary experts is here to satisfy all palates. We also cater to students who have dietary restrictions or special diets such as vegan and vegetarian options. Be sure to check out our event schedule on this website and social media -- we host themed events and meals are jam-packed with excitement and innovation with both taste and engagement!
